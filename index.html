Machi

\Muh-chee\

Literally it is a term for brother-in-law. Used more commonly as an addressal among close friends.

Variations: Macha, Machan

Usage: Machi, pour another round!

Sarakku

\Sir-Ak-Ku\

Tamil slang used for alcohol, booze, liquor.

Usage: For a change let's drink local sarakku!

Da

\Daa\

The south-Indian version of dude.

Usage: What da? Not drinking a?
